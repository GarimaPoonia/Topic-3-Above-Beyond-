DODGE CHALLENGER HELLCAT

The Dodge Challenger SRT comes with a standard 6.2 Litres Supercharged V8 engine. It is only available in rear-wheel drive like its sedan sibling the Dodge Charger. If Dodge Challenger SRT makes its way to India, it will carry a lot higher price tag than the US-based model in the range of 90 Lakhs INR to 1.2 Crores INR.

  • Engine: 6.2L Supercharged SRT V8 Engine

  • Transmission: 6-Speed Manual / 10 Speed Automatic

  • Top Speed: 320 KMPH

  • Fuel Economy: 5.5 KMPL City / 9 KMPL highway
